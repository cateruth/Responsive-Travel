Bienvenue à Montréal

Welcome to Montreal


The largest city in Quebec and the second largest in Canada, this French speaking city is full of historic charm and bustling city life.



Old Montreal

Le Vieux Montréal

The historic neighborhood of Old Montreal is full of attractions, shops and restaurants.

Old Port of Montreal

Vieux-Port de Montréal

Enjoy the riverfront view in the Old Port of Montreal, adjacent to Old Montreal. The Old Port is perfect for many outdoor activities, including the Clock Tower Beach, boating, cycling, roller-blading, and even ice skating!

The Gay Village

Le Village gai

The center of 2SLGBT+ life in Montreal, with the first 2SLGBT+ establishment dating back to 1869! Enjoy the clubs, drag shows, restaurants, and shops in this vibrant scene.

The Montreal Botanical Garden

Jardin botanique de Montréal

190 arces of themed gardens and greenhouses make for spectacular views.
